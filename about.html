Meet Haydee

Hello! I'm Haydee, founder and designer behind FashionHaydee. Jewelry is more than an accessory—it's a language of emotion. I started this studio in Guangzhou to help people tell their stories through custom pieces that are heartfelt, symbolic, and unique.

Our Workshop

Our studio is nestled in the heart of Guangzhou, home to a talented team of artisans who specialize in silver and gold jewelry crafting. From hand carving wax to stone setting and polishing, each piece is made with intention and care. Whether you're celebrating love, memory, or a milestone, we craft pieces that hold meaning.

What We Believe

FashionHaydee is built on the belief that jewelry is a totem of emotion. Every gemstone carries a story. Every design reflects a moment. That’s why we work closely with our clients, transforming their ideas into wearable art that lasts generations. No templates. No shortcuts. Just authentic, beautiful storytelling in metal and stone.
